Eddies Farm

Eddie is an old farmer who lives far far away. He was a normal farmer like everyone else that owns a farm. Until one day something happened to his farm animals. Their sounds were exchanged between all the animals on the farm. There is a monster living in the barn that is throwing hay bails to Eddie the farmer, to keep him away. He needs your help to try and get to the barn. He needs to avoid the obstacles sent by the monster. Help Eddie the farmer recover the correct sounds for each of his animals.
